Important keys and shortcuts
Edit on GitHub

The most important keys and shortcuts of your phone are listed here:

Table 1. Important keys
Keys on your phone keyboard
Send
  • Make a call
  • Answer a call
End/Power
  • End a call
  • Return to the home menu
Lock
  • Lock or unlock the phone
Escape
  • Close the menu
  • Return to previous menu
Menu
  • Open the main menu
  • Select an item on click
Table 2. Phone shortcuts
Most used shortcuts  
Check your voice mail Press and hold 1.
Change your ring tone Press and hold and then select Phone Ring Tones.
Turn on or turn off the speakers Press the key.
Set the device on silent mode Press and hold the key.
Contact speed dial Press and hold the key you would like to assign to contact for speed dial.
